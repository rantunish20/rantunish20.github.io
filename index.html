Let's Propagate Plants!

Find the Node

Pick the plant you want to propagate and find the node.

Cut the node

With a scissor cut the node diagnolally

Water Time

Get a small container for the plant and fill it with water. Put the cutting inside and leave it near a bright area.

The Waiting Game

Now we wait for roots to grow. Once we have roots, it can be potted.
